# Aura Health Sub-Section Style Representations

This document showcases the styling, typography details, and UI layout mockups for all major sub-sections of the **Aura Health** platform under the applied **Seafoam & Oceanic Teal** color scheme.

## Typography Design Specs

The platform uses a dual-typeface system to bridge clinical credibility with emotional comfort:
- **Primary Headers & Callouts**: `Outfit` (sans-serif) — Rounded, premium, and humanizing. Used for dashboard metrics, title texts, and highlight numbers.
- **UI Labels & Body Copy**: `Inter` (sans-serif) — Crisp, highly readable, and professional. Used for settings, tables, forms, logs, and chat messages.

## Design System Integration

### 1. Visual Symbolism
* **The Female Profile Silhouette**: Captures personal care, individual wellness, and human-centric design.
* **The Rhythmic Wellness Wave**: Represents the cycle calendar rhythm, menstrual phase tracking, cardiovascular telemetry, and the flow of active habits.
* **The Gradient Transition**: Smooth blend between the primary Deep Ocean Teal and the accent Seafoam Mint represents physiological transitions, cycles, and restorative balance.

### 2. Brand Typography & Color Alignment
* **"Her" (Bold)**: Written in **Abyss Teal** (`#225156`), emphasizing strength, foundational medical stability, and premium clinical structure.
* **"Rhythm" (Medium/Regular)**: Written in **Seafoam Mint** (`#89C2BF`), highlighting fluid dynamics, soft habit tracking, and responsive health cadence.
* **Font Recommendation**: Outfit Sans or geometric modern Inter for high clarity in UI headers and branding layouts.
